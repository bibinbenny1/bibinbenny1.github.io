My Philosophy

How I Think

I approach problems from first principles. I deconstruct the goal, identify the core components, and build upwards. It's about understanding the 'what' and 'why' before getting lost in the 'how'.

My Approach to Building

I believe in iterative development. Start with a solid foundation, build the core feature, and then expand. Each cycle of building, testing, and refining adds a layer of robustness and clarity.

Principles I Follow

Clarity, efficiency, and purpose. Code should be readable. Systems should be efficient. And every project should have a clear, meaningful goal that guides its development.

What I’m Learning Now

I am deepening my understanding of Python's async capabilities, exploring advanced data analysis techniques, and studying the architecture of large-scale AI systems to solve more complex, real-world problems.
